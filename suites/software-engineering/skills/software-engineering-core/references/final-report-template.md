# Final Report Template

Use this before accepted completion for substantial software-engineering work.

```md
# Final Report: <task name>

## Objective

## What Changed

## Files Touched

## Decisions

## Verification

## Observed Evidence

## Intended State

## Observed State

## Intent Conformance

Use `conforms`, `authorized deviation`, or `unresolved deviation` and name the authority for every accepted delta.

## Mechanism Validity

- `Mechanism Claims Verified`
- `Mechanism Evidence`
- `Unverified Mechanisms`
- `Mechanism Verdict` — `established`, `conditional`, `unproven`, or `contradicted`

## Acceptance Coverage

| Commitment | Type | Source | Expected State | Observed State | Status | Evidence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

## Deviations

Record expected state, observed state, affected commitment, authority, and resolution. Write `none observed` only after comparison.

## Remaining Risks

## Resume Or Follow-up Notes
```
